About Model Additional Scheduled Task Plans
What are Model Additional Plans?

Model additional scheduled task plans define maintenance requirements that are specific to particular make/model combinations. These plans supplement the base asset type plans with model-specific maintenance needs.

When to Use Model Plans?
  • Model-specific maintenance requirements
  • Additional maintenance beyond asset type plans
  • Manufacturer-specific maintenance schedules
  • Model-specific component replacements
